Who Am I?

I was born and raised in the “Land of the Midnight Sun” in the Southeast Alaskan town of Ketchikan. Every summer it was work, fishing and baseball, always in a different order.

I served an LDS Mission to Bogotá, Colombia and have been improving my Spanish every day since then.

I have a large and wonderful family that adds color and excitement to every day.

I try every day to: Give a 100% to every endeavor. Be teachable and strive for a higher standard of excellence. Be honest in all I do. Treat others as I’d like to be treated. Be grateful for what I have so that I don’t take anything for granted. Love unconditionally.

I’ll leave what is put on my tombstone to others, but I hope they will unanimously agree “Corey did the best he could at all times, in all places and for all people.”
